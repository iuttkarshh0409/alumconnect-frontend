# 🚀 Student Dashboard: The Career Launchpad
## (UI Component: `StudentDashboard.js`)

The **Student Dashboard** is designed as a "High-Velocity Launchpad." Its objective is to minimize friction between the student and professional opportunities, making the global alumni network feel accessible and actionable.

---

### 🏛️ 1. Professional Launchpad Header
*   **Aesthetic**: Focused & Minimalist.
*   **Elements**:
    *   **Personalization**: Dynamically greets the student with their name and department.
    *   **Quick Actions**: Real-time shortcuts to search the directory or update their profile.
    *   **Global Navigation**: Seamless links to the Mentorship Hub and Referral Track.

### 📊 2. Referral & Application Pipeline 
*   **The "Pulse" Metric**: Real-time status update of how many referrals are active.
*   **Functional Goal**: To provide immediate psychological reward when a student sees a status transition from "Applied" to "Referred."
*   **Design**: Uses high-contrast status badges (Emerald for Success, Blue for In-Progress).

### ⏳ 3. Mentorship Countdown Console
*   **Urgency Logic**: A visual "card" that highlights the next scheduled mentorship session.
*   **Functional Goal**: To ensure no professional connection is missed. It acts as a "ticking clock" to keep the student engaged.

### 🛰️ 4. Discovery Sidebar
*   **Discovery Mode Utility**: A mini-version of the directory search, allowing the student to search for specific companies (e.g., "Google," "Tesla") without leaving their dashboard.
*   **Skill Clusters**: A placeholder for tracking which skills are currently "trending" among the alumni network.

### 📝 5. Mentorship Intake Stream
*   **Actionable Feed**: Lists incoming messages or pending session approvals from alumni.
*   **Design**: Clean dossier-style rows with "Quick-Reply" or "Add to Calendar" buttons.

---
> **Design Philosophy**: The Student UI uses **Energetic Blues** and **Amber Accents** to symbolize growth, transition, and optimism.
