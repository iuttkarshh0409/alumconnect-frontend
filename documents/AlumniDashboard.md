# 🏛️ Alumni Dashboard: The Mentor Console
## (UI Component: `AlumniDashboard.js`)

The **Alumni Dashboard** is the "Professional Authority Console." It's designed to balance community contribution (mentorship) with visibility (community reputation) in a premium, data-dense environment.

---

### 🏛️ 1. Professional Authority Header
*   **Aesthetic**: Refined & Authoritative.
*   **Elements**:
    *   **Visibility Toggle**: A real-time toggle that lets the Alum set their status to "Open to Mentorship" or "Incognito" (Incognito hides them from students).
    *   **Availability Indicators**: Shows their current capacity for new referrals or calls.

### 🏆 2. Community Impact Tracker (NPI - Alumni Edition)
*   **NPI Scope**: For an alumnus, this is based on their **Referral Quality**, **Response Rate**, and **Endorsements (Kudos)** received.
*   **Functional Goal**: To provide the alumnus with a "Gravitas score" to incentivize active mentorship.
*   **Visual**: A premium gold-accented progress card with a high-contrast hero score.

### 📡 3. Mentorship Intake Hub
*   **Aesthetic**: Streamlined & Efficient.
*   **Features**:
    *   **Student Inquiries**: A clean table or list of students who have requested a referral or mentorship chat.
    *   **Status Management**: Rapid actions (Approve, Snooze, Pass) with built-in templates.
    *   **Student Profile Previews**: Quick-look cards showing a student's NPI score and department.

### 📅 4. Engagement Calendar
*   **Session Scheduler**: A placeholder for a visual timeline of all upcoming video calls or mentorship chats.
*   **Availability Block**: Effortless blocking of professional calendar slots for the AlumConnect network.

### 💖 5. Kudos & Endorsement Feed
*   **Feedback Loop**: A real-time stream of "Kudos" from students.
*   **Functional Goal**: To build a "Wall of Love" to show the Alumnus their impact on the community.

---
> **Design Philosophy**: The Alumni UI uses **Deep Navy tones** and **Gold accents** to symbolize established success, mentorship, and authority.
